About us

Stone Paving & Concrete Finishing, we are a contracting business that specialises in providing top quality commercial and residential stone paving and concrete services. As part of our service, we work with bricks, blocks, tiles, concrete, steel fixing, interior & exterior plastering as well as form works.

We have a team of highly skilled blokes that can fix all your paving & concrete needs as well as anything in between. As part of our offering, we make sure to find the best solution without breaking the bank!

With over 20 years experience in the industry, you can be rest assured that we can get it done from concept to completion. In additon, we will make sure to provide you with the best advice to help you complete your requirements.

Services

Stone Paving, Concrete & Granite

We provide excellent paving services. we work with granite cobbles, natural stone, permeable paving systems. In addition, we also provide paving repairs and restorations.

Tiling, Plastering & Paint works

We provide services on commercial and residentail tiles layering, interior & exterior plastering. We can also offer you services with your painting and finishing requirements.

Brick & Siteworks

We pride ourselves on installing and repairing brickstones. We also provide retaining wall solutions from garden retaining to structural retaining walls. We work with concrete keystone walling and concrete block walls with a natural or plaster finish.
